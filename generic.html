ABOUT AMBE DELAND Che

Ambe Deland is a data enthusiast who has experience in the fields of data analytics, data visualization, data management, project realization, storytelling, policy analysis and healthcare management. As someone transitioning from healthcare into data analytics, I bring a unique blend of domain knowledge and analytical skills to the table (Microsoft Excel, Power Bi, Tableau and SQL). My experience in healthcare provides me with a deep understanding of challenges involved in other industries, trends, and opportunities. By so doing, I possess a keen eye for extracting valuable insights from complex datasets coupled with proficiency in statistical analysis and data visualization, as a result this enables me to transform raw data into actionable information. My journey into data analytics is fueled by a passion for leveraging data to drive meaningful change and improve outcomes. Drawing from my experience in the healthcare industry, I recognize the importance of accurate, reliable and timely data collection, robust analysis, and actionable insights in optimizing key processes and decision-making. My ability to communicate findings from complex healthcare data into understandable insights equips me to collaborate effectively with stakeholders across multidisciplinary teams.

I am committed to learning continuously and develop better skills as I eagerly embrace new tools, techniques, and methodologies in data analytics. My goal is to harness the power of data to streamline operations, and drive innovation within multiple ecosystems. Armed with both healthcare expertise and data analytics proficiency, I am poised to make a significant impact in shaping the future.
